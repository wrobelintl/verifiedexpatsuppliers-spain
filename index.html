Finding Professionals When Buying Property in Spain

Buying property in Spain as a foreigner involves navigating a legal and tax system that is very different from your home country. The right professionals save you money, protect you from costly mistakes, and make the process significantly less stressful.

Do I Need a Lawyer When Buying in Spain?

While not legally required, hiring an independent lawyer (not the developer's lawyer) is strongly recommended. A good Spanish property lawyer will verify that the property has no debts, outstanding mortgages, or planning violations before you complete the purchase.

What is NIE and Why Do I Need One?

The NIE (Numero de Identificacion de Extranjero) is a tax identification number required for all foreigners who want to buy property, open a bank account, or work in Spain. An immigration lawyer can obtain your NIE for you, typically within 2-4 weeks.

What is Non-Resident Tax in Spain?

If you own property in Spain but are not a Spanish tax resident, you are subject to non-resident income tax (IRNR) even if you do not rent out the property. A Spanish tax advisor can file your annual declaration and ensure you are compliant.
